IoT based Gas Leakage Monitoring system

Background

Gas leakage is a big problem in today’s world. Due to lack of proper leakage monitoring. Without monitoring These leaks can lead to physical symptoms and, in some cases, the gas can cause carbon monoxide poisoning in people and animals. This problem leads to the design of a product which monitors gas leakage in real time.There are lots of IoT devices nowadays to monitor the gas leakage over the internet. Experts are also taking advantage of these smart devices to keep an eye on their plants. With tons of new monitoring technology start-ups, IoT is rapidly revolutionizing the industry.

project idea

Gas leakage is a serious problem. Small gas leaks may not have a smell or other physical signs which make it a serious unexpected disaster. Nowadays it is observed in many places like residences and industries. It is noticed that due to gas leakage dangerous accidents occur. There are lots of IoT devices nowadays to monitor gas leakage over the internet.

Here in this project, we will make an IoT based gas leakage Monitoring System which records any occurance of gas leakage and also send an alert whenever there is any leakage. gas leakage alerts could be sent to anywhere in the world over the internet.

Approach

  • In this system, the Intel Galileo board Gen 2 is used for collecting, processing and transmitting all data.
  • Different sensors are used for measuring different parameters.

Components Required

Hardware:

  • Galileo board Gen 2
  • GAS sensor MQ5
  • GAS sensor MQ5
  • Male-female wires Other different sensors will be added to the project

Software:

  • Proteus
  • VS code
